Chapter I

Study Population Overview

This study enrolled 166 CKD patients across multiple Chi Mei hospitals, improving real-world representativeness.
A mean age of 66.5 years reflects a multimorbid, high-risk cohort aligned with real-world clinical care.
A median UACR of 610.5 mg/g indicates that the cohort was dominated by patients with marked albuminuria.
Baseline eGFR was 43.5 ± 24.9 mL/min/1.73m², covering both early and advanced CKD stages.
